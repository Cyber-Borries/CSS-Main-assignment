Who am I?

My name is Adriaan Bornman, I am a South African and lived and worked in Peru for 5 years. I am fully trilingual in Afrikaans, English and Spanish. Which means I can create a website in different languages for you.
I am a web developer with a passion to make the web more accessible to everybody. I am a friendly person with good communication skills and a desire to meet your expectations.
I am proficient in HTML, CSS and JavaScript. Hang on, what does that mean?? That means that I can make your website in the 3 most popular technologies available to us currently, meaning that we are able to create whatever web application you might need.
My favorite things to do when I'm not coding is playing PC games online with my friends, or having a good time with my family. I'm also a motorbike enthusiast.
